### Features Explained

#### 1. **Image Counter**
- Shows: `(Current / Total)`
- Example: `(1 / 31)` means you're viewing image 1 out of 31
- Displayed in **gold color** at the bottom

#### 2. **Circular Navigation**
- When you reach the **last image** and click **right arrow**, it goes back to the **first image**
- When you're at the **first image** and click **left arrow**, it goes to the **last image**

#### 3. **Image Information**
- Each image shows:
  - **Name**: Title of the Gospel reading
  - **Date**: Full date (e.g., "JUNE 1, 2025")
  - **Position**: Your current position in the gallery

### Tips & Tricks

#### For Fast Navigation:
- Use **keyboard arrows** for quick browsing
- Hold down arrow keys to move through images rapidly

#### For Detailed Viewing:
- Click anywhere outside the image to close
- Use the **X button** in the top-right for precise closing

#### On Mobile:
- **Tap left side** of screen: Previous image (via left arrow)
- **Tap right side** of screen: Next image (via right arrow)
- **Pinch to zoom**: View image details (browser default)
- **Double-tap**: Quick zoom in/out (browser default)

### Common Workflows

#### Workflow 1: Daily Gospel Reading
1. Go to "Gospel Toons English" section
2. Click the current month (e.g., "JUNE 2025")
3. Click on today's date image
4. Browse previous/next days using arrows

#### Workflow 2: Explore a Month
1. Select a month from the month navigation
2. Click first image to open lightbox
3. Use **right arrow** to view all images in sequence
4. Press **Escape** when done

#### Workflow 3: Sacred Gallery Exploration
1. Click on a category (e.g., "Mary")
2. Click any image in the category
3. Navigate through all images in that category
4. Close and select another category

### Troubleshooting

**Q: Arrows not showing?**
- A: Arrows only appear when there are multiple images (2+)
- Single images won't show navigation

**Q: Keyboard shortcuts not working?**
- A: Make sure the lightbox is open and focused
- Click inside the lightbox area first

**Q: Image not loading?**
- A: Placeholder image will show automatically
- Check your internet connection
- Reload the page if needed

**Q: How to zoom in?**
- A: Use browser's built-in zoom (Ctrl/Cmd + mouse wheel)
- Or double-click the image (browser default)
